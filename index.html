Privacy Policy for Conference Guard

Welcome to Conference Guard, the Android app designed to automatically activate Do Not Disturb (DND) during conferencing meetings. We prioritize your privacy and are committed to safeguarding your personal information. This Privacy Policy outlines how we handle your data.

Permissions Required

To deliver its functionality, the app requires the following permissions:

  • Usage Access: Monitors phone usage to detect conference calls.
  • Do Not Disturb Access: Enables automatic activation of Do Not Disturb mode.
  • Read Call Log: Accesses call history to identify called number and use it for sending an auto-reply.
  • Receive Calls: Manages incoming calls and performs rejections as needed during a meeting.
  • SMS Permission: Automatically sends an SMS after rejecting a call when the user is in a meeting.

Data Collection and Use

Offline Functionality

- Local Data Storage: The app operates offline, and all data remains on your device. No data is sent to external servers.

- No Sign-Up or Sign-In: No account creation or sign-in is required. No personal data is collected.

Optional Data Sharing

- Statistical Information: Future updates may include an option to share anonymous usage statistics to help us improve the app. Participation is optional, and only aggregated, non-personally identifiable data will be collected if you choose to opt-in.

Data Security

We implement measures to protect your data. Since all data is stored locally on your device, it remains secure and inaccessible to external parties.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy periodically. Changes will be posted here, and we encourage you to review it regularly. Continued use of the app signifies acceptance of the updated policy.
